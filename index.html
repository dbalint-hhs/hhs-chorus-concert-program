The HHS Music Parents Organization

The Hamden High School Music Parents Organization is a non-profit, parent-led organization that supports the Hamden High Band, Orchestra, and Choral Music programs.

We support our students and their teachers by fundraising money for programs and to help students pay for apparel, trips, and equipment.
